Divinity - Features What makes it the ultimate gaming keyboard?

Doubleshot Injection Keycaps - Long lasting durability that is expected of a premium mechanical keyboard. Never worry about your keys during those heavy gaming sessions.

Aluminium Chassis - For extra durability and a premium feel to the keyboard. Helps makes the Divinity Spill Resistant.

USB Pass-through Port - Easy access to a USB 2.0 port on the keyboard itself. Plug in your headset or thumb drive without having to reach behind your desktop.

Detachable Wrist Rest - For additional comfort, the Divinity includes a wrist rest that can be detached depending on your preference.

16 Million Colors - Customize each key's backlight to your exact liking with breathing, cycling, fading, or steady lighting. Save up to 3 profiles to the onboard memory and keep them available even when you switch computers.

Windows Lock - Keep the Windows key from accidentally minimizing your screen during a critical moment of your game. Simply lock & unlock the Windows key.
