Making a Reddit Bot with Node.js

Reddit is a magical place on the Internet that can make hours and hours of a person's life mysteriously disappear. On Reddit, there are many webpages, or subreddits, dedicated to people's various interests. For example, starving college students might go on /r/FoodPorn to look at pictures of delicious food. Fans of Netflix's Stranger Things might go on /r/StrangerThings to discuss their favorite theory. Have some spare time? Procrastinating on a big project? Spend some time on Reddit, and browse to your heart's content.

Today, we will be learning how to work with Reddit's API using Node.js. We will do so by making a Reddit bot that can perform simple actions, such as creating and upvoting posts.
